The individual files contains
GAR_GTM_IUGG_presentation_updated_distr  : A general presentation of the latest work for the Global Assessment Report (GAR) and the ideas to extend and generalize this work to a Global Tsunami Model (GTM). Presentation held at the IUGG.
GTM_meeting_presentation_distr : A presentation of the outline of the GTM held at the first scoping meeting during the IUGG.
IOC_TOWS_GTM_distr : Presentation of GTM for the IOC-TOWS working group.
noteToTOWS_taskteam : A short document summarizing GTM aims relevant for the IOC TOWS working group.
